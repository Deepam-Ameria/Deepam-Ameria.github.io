04 · Optimal Control

Model-Based Control for Humanoid Soccer

QP + Direct Collocation + IHLQR/TVLQR for target-directed kicking on Unitree G1

  • Developed a model-based control framework for humanoid soccer kicking in the Unitree G1 simulation environment, integrating whole-body planning with accurate ball-strike execution
  • Formulated a Quadratic Program (QP) to compute the optimal foot impulse magnitude, direction, and contact point for target-directed ball strikes given desired shot trajectories
  • Generated dynamically feasible kicking trajectories using Direct Collocation trajectory optimization, enforcing joint limits, ground contact complementarity, and timing constraints
  • Implemented IHLQR and TVLQR for whole-body balance stabilization and accurate trajectory tracking during the pre-kick, contact, and follow-through phases
