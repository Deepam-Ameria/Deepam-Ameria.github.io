Model-Based Control for Humanoid Soccer

QP + Direct Collocation + IHLQR/TVLQR for target-directed kicking on Unitree G1

  • Formulated a QP to compute optimal foot impulse, direction, and contact point for target-directed ball strikes
  • Generated feasible kicking trajectories using Direct Collocation, enforcing joint limits and ground contact constraints
  • Implemented IHLQR and TVLQR for balance and trajectory tracking through kick phases
