About Me

Passionate about robotics and innovation

I work across autonomous robots, electric vehicles, and full-stack systems, from hardware to software and from concept to deployment.

Currently, I'm developing Lunar ROADSTER as part of my capstone project under the guidance of Dr. William "Red" Whittaker. It involves making an autonomous lunar rover that can groom the craters and create traversable surface trails for future space exploration. This project perfectly embodies my vision of robotics advancing human capabilities beyond Earth's boundaries.

My work focuses on creating autonomous systems that can operate in extreme environments and learn from their interactions with the world. I'm particularly interested in the intersection of mechanical engineering, controls, and cutting-edge AI.
